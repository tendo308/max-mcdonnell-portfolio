About me

Hey again,

I’m a game designer interested in mechanics, level design, programming, and world building. Having recently graduated from RMIT’s Bachelor of Design (Games), I’m excited to start my career.

Some of my favourite games include: Bloodborne, Terraria, Dark Souls, Portal 2, and ULTRAKILL.
